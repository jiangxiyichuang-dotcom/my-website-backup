How to Make Smart Whiteboard More Useful

Views: 0     Author: Site Editor     Publish Time: 2023-01-02      Origin: Site

Inquire

The reason why the smart whiteboard is used as one of the multimedia teaching equipment in schools is that the smart whiteboard itself has the advantages that the traditional blackboard + chalk does not have, and it can also reduce the workload of teachers and improve the interaction between teachers and students.


Here is the content list:

  • Scenario Design

  • Brainstorming

  • Smart whiteboard demonstrates the experimental effect


Scenario design

Combining the advantages of the smart whiteboard and the characteristics of kindergarten students, several scenarios need to be designed. Scenario design research shows that it is a socialization process that can implicitly promote cooperation among students. Therefore, some interactive content and processes are designed in the smart whiteboard to help students improve the cooperative model. At the same time, through discussions among students, each person expresses his ideas and then combines all the answers to integrate them into an effective answer. Thereby enhancing the students' independent thinking and cooperation to solve problems. Design different scene modes through the smart whiteboard, so that students can freely group and discuss. By discovering and debating problems, students can synthesize everyone's opinions and then summarize a set of answers. Then the teacher summarizes the answers given by each group of students and analyzes its strengths and weaknesses so that students can grow their knowledge base and have a deeper understanding and cognition of the problem.


Brainstorming

Students get different ideas and ideas through intense brainstorming, allowing students to discuss and cooperate to solve problems, thereby improving the efficiency of the smart whiteboard. It can make the original boring knowledge more vivid, flexible, and interesting, and at the same time, it can also exercise the students' ability of expression and logical thinking, and make the situation theory more effective. Transform the subconscious into the conscious through the abstraction and systematization of articulating knowledge. Many kindergartens have bilingual teaching in small classes. If only writing words on the blackboard and reading textbooks are used, this single teaching mode will inevitably make children feel boring. If you use the smart whiteboard, you can let the children read English with the smart whiteboard by watching English animation, English children's songs, and English stories to improve their English interaction ability.


Smart whiteboard demonstrates the experimental effect

Some materials in the chemistry experiment class are inflammable and explosive objects. If there are mistakes in the operation of the students, it may cause a fire or an explosion accident. If the mistakes made by the students are not so serious, then it may skew the experimental data, ruin the whole experiment, etc. A smart whiteboard can also deepen the cognitive structure, and traditional teaching will inevitably make students feel boring in class. However, if you use the smart whiteboard, you can play interactive teaching courses that students are more interested in, and it can also allow students to have more interaction with teachers so that students are more interested in learning.

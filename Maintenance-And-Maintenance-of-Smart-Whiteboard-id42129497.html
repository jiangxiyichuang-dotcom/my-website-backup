Maintenance And Maintenance of Smart Whiteboard

Views: 0     Author: Site Editor     Publish Time: 2024-04-24      Origin: Site

Inquire

The most important thing in the maintenance and maintenance of the smart whiteboard is the maintenance of the board surface. The board is the main part and important display part of the smart whiteboard. Its maintenance needs to start from two aspects: daily maintenance and cleaning.


Here is the content list:

l Maintenance of smart whiteboard

l Cleaning of smart whiteboard


Maintenance of smart whiteboard

In the process of using the infrared smart whiteboard, it is best not to let anything other than the pointer or writing instrument come into contact with the whiteboard. This is mainly because the contact with the smart whiteboard is too diverse or once it is used, it can cause damage to the board surface. As a result, the smart whiteboard projection will have some influence on the display when it is used in the future. Since the board surface of a smart whiteboard is generally made of high-quality wear-resistant materials, there will be no problems in normal use, but it is necessary to avoid contacting the board surface with sharp objects to avoid damaging and scratching the board surface. In addition, under normal circumstances, do not leave the writing on the board for a long time, so that the writing is not easy to clean. And it is especially important to note that during the use of the smart whiteboard, you cannot use oil-based crayons to write on the smart whiteboard. If this happens, you must immediately wipe the surface of the smart whiteboard with absorbent cotton dipped in alcohol.


Cleaning of smart whiteboard

To ensure a good display effect of the board surface, the board surface needs to be cleaned frequently. If the smart whiteboard is used frequently, it is best to clean it once a week or so to maintain the best clarity. The user is reminded that the interactive smart whiteboard should first exit the system before cleaning the smart whiteboard panel. Because touching the screen while the computer is in another state, applying pressure to the screen can activate programs or clutter icons. After exiting the system, put the projector into standby mode, which makes it easier to show smudges and streaks. When moving the smart whiteboard, do not touch the screen or wipe the whiteboard. Generally speaking, the cleaning of the writing surface of the smart whiteboard can be done with a clean soft cloth. Never wipe the screen with a dry cloth to avoid the electrostatic charge. It can also be used after trying non-abrasive denatured alcohol and glass cleaner on a small area. Special smart whiteboard cleaner can achieve a better cleaning effect, if you encounter difficult-to-remove dirt, you can spray the cleaner on the panel, and then wipe it with a paper towel. And don't worry if you get more stubborn marks, just cover them completely with ink from a dry-erase marker, then wipe them off with a soft cloth. Dry-erase inks contain solvents that dissolve permanent inks. If traces of the original permanent ink remain, spray the area with a standard glass cleaner or whiteboard cleaner and wipe away. When wiping dirt, do not use thinner and volatile oil to wipe the writing surface of the smart whiteboard, otherwise, discoloration or damage to the writing surface may occur. Before cleaning, it is best to check the instructions provided with the smart whiteboard equipment.

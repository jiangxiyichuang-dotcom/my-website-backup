Yichuang products will participate in the 2021 Jiangxi International Mobile Internet of Things Expo

Views: 0     Author: Site Editor     Publish Time: 2022-03-16      Origin: Site

Inquire

The 2021 Jiangxi International Mobile Internet of Things Expo will be held in Yingtan from November 8th to 9th.With the theme of 'Intelligent Integration of All Things Wins the Future', we will discuss and discuss hot issues in the fields of global 5G and Internet of Things technology development trends and intelligent applications, and jointly promote the integration of mobile Internet development, economy and society.Jiangxi Yichuang Electronic Technology Co., Ltd. was honored to be invited by Yichun Science and Technology Bureau to participate in this exhibition as the only intelligent manufacturing enterprise representing the Yichun area in Fengcheng.


The fair is divided into indoor and outdoor exhibition areas.The indoor exhibition area includes City Hall, Brand Hall, and Enterprise Hall.223 exhibitors (enterprises) and an outdoor exhibition area display 03 characteristic brand applications such as smart water conservancy, smart 119, smart policing, smart medical care, and smart manufacturing, as well as the latest technological achievements of related companies, equipped with 5G+ AI tumor screening vehicles, 5G Interactive experience projects such as remote consultation, epidemic prevention and distribution robots, and 5G exhibition vehicles.In the indoor exhibition area, there is a national brand pavilion. Huawei, ZTE, Inspur and three major communication trainers from China Telecom, China Mobile and China Unicom will participate in the exhibition.The indoor city pavilion is divided into prefecture-level cities and city pavilions, bringing together high-tech enterprises in local cities and displaying the high-tech achievements of each enterprise.

In this expo, Jiangxi Yichuang Technology Electronics Co., Ltd. takes 'Internet of Everything, Smart Education' as its theme, highlighting China's modern science and technology teaching and conferences.Yichuang Technology fully demonstrated the application scenarios of deep integration brought by new products to education and teaching.Yichuang Technology will continue to devote itself to the innovation and focus of educational technology, provide users with practical and easy-to-use educational informatization application tools and cutting-edge educational informatization theoretical research results, promote the development of educational informatization in China, and make future education better. Well, it's easier for teachers to teach students.
